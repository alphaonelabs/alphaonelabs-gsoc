About Alpha One Labs

Alpha One Labs is an open-source organization dedicated to building educational technology that empowers learners worldwide.

Our Mission

Democratize education through innovative, accessible, and high-quality open-source learning platforms.

First-Time GSoC

"As a first-time GSoC organization, Alpha One Labs is committed to providing a supportive and enriching environment for students to learn, grow, and make meaningful contributions to open-source."

Tech Stack

We work with HTML, Python, Shell, and modern web technologies to build scalable education platforms.
